DORAEMON

The Doraemon section of the theme park brings the beloved blue robotic cat and his friends to life through fun, interactive attractions. Visitors can explore Nobita’s neighborhood, step inside Doraemon’s secret gadget world, and enjoy exciting rides, 3D shows, and photo zones featuring iconic scenes from the anime. Children can try hands-on activities like Gadget Labs, Time Machine rides, and Treasure hunts inspired by Doraemon’s inventions. The area also includes Doraemon Café serving themed snacks, and merchandise stores filled with exclusive toys and souvenirs — making it a magical experience for fans of all ages.

SPIDERMAN

The Spider-Man zone offers a thrilling adventure inspired by Marvel’s iconic superhero. Visitors can swing into action with high-speed 3D rides and interactive motion simulators that let them experience what it’s like to be Spider-Man battling villains across New York City. The area features life-sized cityscapes, web-slinging challenges, and photo zones with Spider-Man, Venom, and Green Goblin. Guests can also explore Peter Parker’s science lab, enjoy themed food at the Daily Bugle Café, and shop for exclusive Spider-Man toys, costumes, and collectibles. It’s a perfect blend of action, technology, and superhero excitement for all ages.

PINK PANTHER

The "Pink Panther" refers to a 1963 film, a character, a franchise, and even a criminal group. The original film, directed by Blake Edwards, featured a stolen diamond with a flaw resembling a leaping pink panther. The popularity of the bumbling Inspector Jacques Clouseau in that film led to him headlining subsequent films, and the animated cartoon character of the same name was created for the opening credits

MICKEY MOUSE

The Mickey Mouse zone is a joyful and colorful world celebrating Disney’s most iconic character. Visitors can step into Mickey’s Toon Town, where everything looks straight out of a cartoon — from Mickey’s cheerful house to Minnie’s garden and Goofy’s fun play areas. Families can enjoy gentle rides, musical parades, and character meet-and-greets with Mickey, Minnie, Donald, and friends. The area also features live stage shows, photo booths, and a Mickey Café serving themed snacks and desserts. Exclusive Disney merchandise stores offer plush toys, apparel, and souvenirs, creating a magical experience filled with nostalgia, laughter, and imagination for visitors of all ages.
