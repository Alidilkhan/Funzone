ABOUT US

At Fun Zone Theme Park, we believe fun brings people together!

"Fun, excitement, and memories — all in one place!"

MISSION

At Fun ZoneZ, providing a safe and fun environment for friends and families to gather is our main priority. Our goal is to give our guests a unique and exciting experience every time they visit our family friendly fun center. In addition to creating an enjoyable setting, we are committed to ensuring a safe and sanitary place for kids and adults of all ages to enjoy. Our playground and laser tag areas were designed with safety in mind. In addition, we guarantee to provide fresh and top-quality ingredients in all our menu items ensuring a satisfying dining experience in our eating center. Our state-of-the-art facility has a Play structure, Arcade and Laser tag as well as spacious party and event rooms making it perfect for all sorts of parties, fundraisers and even school field trips!

VISION

Fun ZoneZ is dedicated to creating a safe atmosphere for guests of all ages to enjoy and create memories. Our staff and attractions create an environment that combines fun and safety in one place. Our employees are encouraged to interact with guests and contribute to make sure they have the best experience possible. We are continuously training our employees to ensure the most up to date safety guidelines are in place and being applied.

VALUE

Family Fun for Everyone!
We value providing entertainment and attractions for guests of all ages and making sure everyone has a great time at Fun ZoneZ Teamwork equals success!
Every employee contributes to providing a safe and fun environment and are dedicated to ensuring an enjoyable experience for all! “Play, Win, and Celebrate” Attitude!
Our positive attitude and passion for fun is our core value while entertaining our guests. Everyone has a great time creating lifetime memories.

About Fun Zone

Fun Zone Theme Park offers a wide variety of rides, attractions, and entertainment to thrill guests of all ages. Safety and fun are our top priorities.
